Are you curious about palindromes? You're in the right place! PalindromePal is your go-to destination for all things related to palindromes. Whether you're a palindrome enthusiast or just starting to explore the fascinating world of palindromes, we've got something for everyone.

A palindrome is a word or sentence that's spelled the same way both forward and backward, ignoring punctuation, case, and spacing.
